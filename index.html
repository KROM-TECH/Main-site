How it works

1

Sign up and get verified

for record purpose all the users of this platform would need Sign up and get verified in order to hire and work as developers

2

Get tested and ranked

This isn't necessary for employer but in order for employee to get placed in the right jobs they need to be tested and ranked.

3

Rankings

the rankings are periodically checked and updated monthly based on the Task done and rating of the employers and other cateria

4

Payments

You be paid according to your skill level and the employers budget before commencement of the job, pay will be know and you can choose to bargin or refuse tasks. ther are also payment free tasks for beginner level employee to rank up faster

Our Goals

Student/Youth employment

We seek to create more and better employments for students and youths, so they can be more independent and employable before graduation

better employee and employer relationship

This platforms creates an avenue for a company/individual to outsource both students and youth labour for better product delivery

A community of Employable Student

We hope to create forums and chat rooms where students can relax, chat and discuss about their next big project
